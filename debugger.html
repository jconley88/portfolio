Advanced Trace-Based Debugger for Ruby and Rails

An unconventional debugger that offers comprehensive insights by retaining all execution data for higher level analysis.

This project has been a long-term ambition of mine, and in early 2024, I dedicated significant time to developing an alpha version. Unlike traditional debuggers that focus on microscopic inspection of a running program, this tool builds on the fundamentals of a time-traveling debugger to display a breadth of data all at once with higher-level navigation. This approach offloads the cognitive burden of understanding your code's broader context, providing comprehensive views of variable values and interactions, while still retaining the detailed insights needed to pinpoint an issue.

The debugger is now fully functional, and I am preparing for a public release after additional stress testing. I am eager to share more details about this project and welcome any inquiries or feedback.
